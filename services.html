Understand Your Options

There are more than 20 different visa categories as well as green card categories. Each category has its own requirements, benefits and restrictions. We will help you find the one that is most suitable to your situation, and guide you through the application process.

Our services include the followings:

  • Providing consultation on your options and building application strategies.
  • Gathering and drafting necessary application forms and supporting documents.
  • Submitting your application to the government and be the point of contact for your case.
  • Keeping track of your case progress and communicating with the government to facilitate the adjudication of your case.
  • Providing consultation before your interview with the government, if an interview is required.
  • Answering any questions or concerns you may have throughout the application process.
